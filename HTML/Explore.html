Details about Rooms and availabilities:

  • You can register and than login to book your rooms for the meetings.
  • you have to mention your credentials with meeting time and date.
  • Small rooms has a capacity of 1-2 members and it consist of less availabilities.
  • Medium rooms has a capacity of 4-5 members and Large rooms has capacity of 8-10-12 members.these two has more availabilities.
  • Also you can order refreshment drinks at the meeting time.
  • Once you have booked, you have option to cancel your booking.
